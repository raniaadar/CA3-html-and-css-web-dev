About

Aberdeen is a port city in northeast Scotland, where the Dee and Don rivers meet the North Sea. With an offshore petroleum industry, the city is home to an international population. It's also known as the Granite City for its many enduring grey-stone buildings.

Attractions

Aberdeen has grown into a city of sprawling Victorian parks, top universities, high-end restaurants, and luxury shopping. It's not all about splashing the cash, though. Explore our Visit Page to find out more about the range of activites available!

Community

In Aberdeen, you can expect a warm welcome no matter where you're from. We are proud of our rich cultural diversity and are striving to create an environment in our city where everyone can feel like they're welcome.
